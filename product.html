Our Process

Premium Black Tea

We source our black tea from the world's best tea-growing regions, ensuring each batch is ethically harvested and meticulously inspected for a rich, robust flavor.

Artful Brewing

Our tea is brewed with precision, steeped at the perfect temperature to extract its full aroma and taste, creating a strong yet smooth base for our milk tea.

Fresh Milk & House-Made Tapioca

We use only the freshest milk, whether dairy or non-dairy, to add a creamy texture. Our house-made tapioca balls, crafted in small batches, offer a delightful chewiness and subtle sweetness.

Harmonious Blend

Every cup of our Black Tea Milk Boba is a harmonious blend of premium ingredients and expert preparation, offering a delicious and satisfying experience.

Indulge in our Black Tea Milk Boba and enjoy the perfect fusion of flavor and texture in every sip.
